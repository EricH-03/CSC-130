Part 1: Items 1-11

  1. Item 1:

    2. The idea for my app is essentially a "Rent-A-Bee" service where local beekeepers are matched with users who would benefit with a beehive in their backyards.

  2. Item 2:

    3. The main 2D aspects are surveys in which the specfic desire of the user is matched with the offering of a beekeeper. There would also be a built in messaging service so that beekeepers and users can be put into contact without exchanging other means of contact.

    The main 3D parts are an AR modeling system where you can visualize the beehive in 3D space inorder to see if it fits size wize or with other decor.

  3. Item 3:

    4. A webapp to facillitate interactions with beekeepers and our users in order to rent out beehives to help pollenate their garden or help sell their eco-friendly life style.

  4. Item 4:

    5. Bee populations have been steadily on the decline for years. Many beekeepers don't have the space or ecological diversity on their properties to support the number of constantly growing hives. Urban enviroments provide the a perfect solution to this and help both the user with their garden and the beekeepers with their issue this win-win also helps the enviroment and hopefully the bee population.

  5. Item 5:

    6. By having a AR system where you can virtually place a beehive and receive feedback on it's location, (ie is it big enough, level enough , etc..) but also imagine how a beehive would look in their backyard. How busy is it? Which way could the bees be flying? All these would immerse our user into my application.

  6. Item 6:

    • This works well in XR because it helps the user visualize
    • This doesn't work well in XR because it adds a certain technolgical barrier of entry
    • It works because it helps immerse the person into this possibility

  7. Item 7:

    • This idea is better in 3D because it helps the user really imagine what it could be like
    • 3D lends the idea credibiltiy, surely no one would put in this much effort if the idea wasn't sound
    • It would make the idea harder to implement

  8. Item 8:

    9. AR is useful to picture the beehive and plan your space accordingly. MR could be useful to interact with the space around the beehive and VR would be great to fully immerse yourself and imagineh how the bee's might interact with the beehive.

  9. Item 9:

    10. Does this idea exist in 2D?
    Yes, this idea exists as most of the infrastructure is all 2D. For example, the built in communication features.

    Does this idea exist in 3D?
    While not existing entirely in 3D there are certainly 3D elements in my project such as the beehive visualization in which 3D is required to properly imagine the beehive on their property.

    What could you do in your project in 3D that you can't do in 2D?
    The visualization of the beehive would be completely impossible in 2D and requires 3D to even be remotely useful.

  10. Item 10:

    11. Useful: because it helps facillitate a useful interaction in which both parties and the bees benefit
    Usable: by keeping the implementation as simple as reasonable, and copying succesful webapps UIs
    Desirable: by incoporating 3D/XR elements we make the experience enjoyable/fun for the users
    Valuable: by helping maintain bee population and providing communites with better pollenated plants
    Findable due to it's unique idea and the fact that it would be targeted at just Victoria, it would be made available in those places.
    Accessible and Inclusive: by ensuring the webapp is compatiable with screen-readers and by focusing on a simple UI

  11. Item 11:

    12. The empathy map!
